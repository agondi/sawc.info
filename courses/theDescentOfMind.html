The Descent of Mind

Course Outline


How does one create language? How differently do animals and humans behave?The story of evolution will develop as a narrative, explaining why man and its successive hominids behave the way they do in certain situations, reacting to stimuli, forming social structures, developing language and gaining sentience. Students will engage with the material through debates, documentaries, case studies and much more.

Learning Objectives

  • Ability to trace the origin of human behaviour

  • Understanding language and its evolution

  • Comprehending similarities and differences between how animals and humans think

  • Correlating how technology and social structures affect our thinking.
